This is a Hotel Management Website. Used to manage bookings (view, search, add, delete, edit, update). Once the room is booked by customers, employee can check in, move, check out and cancel the booked rooms. There is only two ways to pay money; Cash and OnePay. If they are not familiar with OnePay payment, they can choose to pay cash at the cashier. It can also provide various report of revenue (weekly, monthly, yearly...) with detailed graphs making them easier to understand. Website could be changed to 2 languages, English and Lao. The Website has been developed using PHP, Javascript and Bootstrap 5.3.1 with Mysql as database.
